Who are We?

EHTP ElectroPower is a school club from Hassania School of Public Works that specializes in electronics, robotics and programming!

We're a group of passionate engineering students who enjoy tinkering with electronics and delving into the intrecacies of software and hardware design, check out our Instagram for the latest news and announcements.
